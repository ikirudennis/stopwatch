Stopwatch

A simple stopwatch jQuery plug-in. Go ahead and put multiple stopwatches on a page. It works fine. :)

Example:

<div id="clock1"></div>
<div id="clock2" style="float:left"></div>

<script type="text/javascript">
  $('#clock1').stopwatch();
  $('#clock2').stopwatch({'displaytime': true});
</script>
